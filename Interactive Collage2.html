An Interactive Collage

Symbolism, Metaphors, and Allusions

It's truly a rarity to come across texts so imbued with romantic symbolism as those found within the pages of this novel. This uniqueness presents us with an opportunity we cannot afford to miss: the chance to immerse ourselves in the richness of the symbols, metaphors, and allusions that unfold throughout the text. Each page is infused with these elements, bestowing upon the narrative a romantic aura that sets it apart significantly from other books. For this reason, I have compiled in an interactive collage a selection of representative excerpts from various parts of the work, with the aim of illustrating more clearly what I wish to convey.
